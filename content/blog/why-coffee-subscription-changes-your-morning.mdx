## The Problem With Supermarket Coffee

Walk into any supermarket and you'll find shelves of coffee bags with no roast date. That coffee could be 6, 9, even 12 months old. Coffee is at its peak flavour in the first 4 weeks after roasting — after that, it's a slow decline into flatness and bitterness.

Most people have never tasted truly fresh coffee. Once you do, you can't go back.
